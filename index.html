What is HOME?

HOME is a fan-made documentary inspired by Billie Eilish and the people her music has brought together. it tells stories of healing, memories, friendship, and belonging. No matter where we come from, we all deserve a place to call HOME.

⚠️ IMPORTANT — READ BEFORE APPLYING

Submitting the registration form does not mean you've officially joined HOME.

Once HOME 4 or HOME 5 begins production, selected participants will receive a message from me on Instagram.

You'll receive:

• Recording instructions
• Questions to answer
• Submission deadline
• Everything you need to know before filming

You can record however you're comfortable. Whether you show your face, hide it, use voice only or another creative approach depends on the option you selected in your registration form.

After recording, simply send your video before the deadline. Once editing begins, late submissions unfortunately can't be added because production will already be underway.

💙 THE PURPOSE OF HOME

HOME isn't just another Billie Eilish fan project.

It's a place where people can finally speak about the feelings they've hidden for years without feeling guilty, judged or told they're "just seeking attention."

Almost everyone participating in HOME has experienced difficult moments in life. That's what makes this project feel like HOME.

Here, your story matters. Your feelings matter. Your voice matters.

No matter where you're from, you're welcome here.

🌍 HELP US REACH BILLIE
