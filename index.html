Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I fill out the form?

If you're selected for HOME 4 or HOME 5, I'll contact you through Instagram once production begins. You'll receive all the instructions and the questions you'll answer in your video.

Do I have to show my face?

No. Whether you show your face or stay anonymous is completely your choice. I'll follow whatever option you selected in the registration form.

How do I record my video?

Record your responses however you feel most comfortable. There is no required style or camera. Speak naturally and be yourself.

When do I submit it?

Every participant receives a submission deadline. If you miss it, I'll have to begin editing without your video so the project can stay on schedule.

Can I leave HOME later?

Yes. Participation is completely voluntary. If you change your mind before production starts, just let me know.
