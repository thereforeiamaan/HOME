Story

HOME is a global fan project inspired by Billie Eilish. It brings together people from around the world to share memories, emotions, friendships and the feeling of belonging. Every participant carries a different story, but together they create one HOME.

About HOME 2

HOME 2 continues the journey started by HOME. This chapter takes a different approach. Instead of introducing themselves, every participant answers a series of meaningful questions about life, memories, healing, music, and the impact Billie Eilish has had on them. Each answer becomes another piece of the story. Different people. Different perspectives. One HOME.
